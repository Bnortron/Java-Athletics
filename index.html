Certifications & Education

CSEP-CPT

Completed the CSEP - Certified Personal Trainer tests.

Pre-Script L1

Completed Level 1 certification through Pre-Script. Learned about the anatomy and the mechanics of the body.

DTS - Kettlebells

Completed the Kettlebells coach certification through DTS. Learned how to use a kettlebell in exercises.

DTS - Mini Core

Completed the Mini Core certification through DTS. Learned how to teach clients to engage their core in different methods and learned how to keep core engaged in certain exercises.

ETA - L1

Completed the Level 1 certification through ETA. Learned different assessments, and protoals to help clientele.

Algonquin College - FHP

Completed the 2 year program, Fitness and Health Promotion to gain the base knowledge of how to assess, program and train clients.

GGS - Pre & Post Natal

Completed the Pre & Post Natal certification through the GGS. Learn about the nutrition, adaptation and exercises that women can/cannot do during both pre and post natal.

Niagara College - NSP

Completed the 1 year program, Nutrition for Sport and Performance, to gain the knowledge on how to assess, research and plan a nutrition program for athletes.
